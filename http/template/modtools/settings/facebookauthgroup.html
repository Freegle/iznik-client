Link to Facebook Group

You can link one or more Facebook "buy and sell" groups to your Freegle group. This helps more people find out about Freegle.

OFFERs/WANTEDs from the Freegle group which have been posted from Freegle Direct will be posted onto the Facebook group. People have to sign up to Freegle and will join your group if they reply. We can't post messages from other sources - we don't have permissions.

You don't have to be a Page Admin on the Facebook group, you can just be a normal member. The group has to be a 'Public' rather than 'Closed' group (just because that's what Facebook lets us do). You want to choose busy groups, because then we are reaching new people.
