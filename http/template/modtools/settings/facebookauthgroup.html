Link to Facebook Group

You can link a Facebook "buy and sell" group to your Freegle group. This helps more people find out about Freegle.

OFFERs/WANTEDs from the Freegle group will be posted onto the Facebook group. People have to sign up to Freegle and will join your group if they reply.

You don't have to be a Page Admin on the Facebook group, you can just be a normal member.

The posting will only work if you are using Chrome with the ModTools plugin installed.
