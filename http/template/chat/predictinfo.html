How should you choose?

How someone replies can tell you a bit about them. Once you've freegled a bit, you'll have instincts about who to choose. That's part of the fun!

To help if you're new, we suggest which replies might work out better than others. We always recommend waiting a bit - someone lovely might reply later.

How does this work? The and buttons at the bottom of the screen let you show how you feel about other freeglers. We analyse which kinds of replies make people give a thumbs up or down later. It's not a guarantee, but it's pretty accurate.
