Volunteering Opportunities

These are local opportunities, posted by other freeglers like you. If you are a charity or good cause that needs volunteers, ask our lovely community of freeglers if they can help!

Freegle is also always looking for more volunteers - for local communities, graphics, publicity, improving the website...there's lots to do. If you'd like to get involved, contact your local community.

To post volunteering opportunities, you first need to join one of the communities in your local area.
