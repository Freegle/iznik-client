Working with your local Freegle volunteers

Each Freegle community is run by volunteers and they are there to help you and the members of that community get the best out of Freegle. Keeping in touch with them can really help build a good relationship and ultimately benefit your council.

Your local volunteer can post events and messages on behalf of your council, help with your waste reduction campaigns and they may even be able to attend any events you may be running.

You can find their contact details on your local group pages, or email councils@ilovefreegle.org and we can provide you with them.
