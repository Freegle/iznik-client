Can you help get more people freegling?

"Business cards" are a great way to get more people freegling - you can hand them out, or stick up on noticeboards, in cafes etc. They look like this:

Would you like some?

One of our lovely volunteers can post them to you. A couple of things first:

  • We can send these if you pay £1 via PayPal to cover the cards, packaging and postage. We'd love to do it for free, but at the moment we just can't afford to.
  • We package these up carefully - but very occasionally things can go wrong and Royal Mail may ask you for extra postage. If this would be very annoying then it's best not to ask for them - but if it happens, just don't pay. It's better for us to lose some cards than for you to have to pay postage.

Please give us a postal address to send them to.

For larger numbers, you'll need to order them directly.

You can do this from Moo - they're not the cheapest, but we've uploaded the design. Register and click to create a product.

Order from Moo

Or you can download the design and then upload it to other sites (e.g. Vistaprint or Instantprint). A bit more effort, but cheaper.
