Please help keep Freegle running!

Freegle is free to use, but not free to run.

With around 1000 local volunteers we support more than 2 million members and save more than a million pounds worth of goods each year from going to landfill or incinerators. But we have to pay for some IT and publicity costs.

If you can afford a small donation and have found Freegle useful, please send a pound or two our way.
