Aviva Community Fund

Help promote Freegle in your community by voting for Freegle at Aviva

You can help get the Freegle message out by voting for Freegle in Aviva Community Fund before midday on 21st November 2017. We're asking for funding to help the 400+ Freegle local groups do more publicity, in particular working more with local councils.

Vote for Freegle Now!
The Aviva site may be busy and slow, but do persist!

You will need to register for free and then you have ten votes to cast on projects - there are lots of good causes there, but please consider Freegle! You can also enter a prize draw.

If we get through this round of voting then we are one of the finalists - judges will make the final choice with the winners announced on 16th January 2018. If we're successful then we'll be working on this project throughout 2018. Thanks to our insurers NatureSave for submitting our entry.
