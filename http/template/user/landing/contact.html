How can we help?

Our new site has just gone live! Bear with us. If you think the site is horribly broken, please tell us - mail us with a screenshot if possible.

These are the most common questions we get asked - please have a look and see if this is what you need.

Why has the site changed (again)?

If you've used Freegle Direct before, you'll notice that it's changed. We've done a bit of a revamp; here's why:

  • To work better on mobiles and tablets
  • To make the site easier to use and more friendly
  • So that the code is easier for geek volunteers to maintain and improve

We know it's always a bit of a shock when a website changes, but we hope that after a little while you'll be happily freegling.

I'm getting too many emails

If you go to the Settings Page then you can change how many mails you get in your Mail Settings.

If you want to pause mails for a while, you can do that there too.

Something else?

If your question isn't answered above, then you can contact your volunteer team.

Your local group will usually be the best way for you to get help, and it helps us if you use them where possible. But you can also contact:

  • Our support volunteers at support@ilovefreegle.org. They deal with questions about this site, or problems with freegling where your local group volunteers can't help.
  • Our national mailbox volunteers at info@ilovefreegle.org. They deal with more general questions about Freegle which aren't specific to one group.
  • Our media volunteers at media@ilovefreegle.org. Please use this if you are a member of the media and want to help publicise Freegle. For urgent press enquiries only, call +44 (0)7962 449573.
