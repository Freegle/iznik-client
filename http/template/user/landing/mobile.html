Mobile app

Please try the new Freegle mobile app for phones and tablets. Download using these links or search for Freegle in your app store - it's free!

Please report any issues to support@ilovefreegle.org. The app is only available in the UK app stores.

The app only works for groups on Freegle Direct - about 80% of all groups. It does not work with Norfolk groups or if your group only runs on Yahoo.

  • Google Play for Android™ phones and tablets: Android 4.03 or later
  • AppStore for iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch: iOS 8 or later

The app is currently the same as the main Freegle website - but it's a bit quicker, and you get notifications of replies so you don't have to rely on email.
